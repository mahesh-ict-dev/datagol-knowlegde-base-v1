### Requesting access to workspaces

1.  On the Home page, from the left navigation panel, click Workspace. The Workspace page, which lists all the workspaces is displayed.
    
2.  From the upper right corner, click the Join button.
    
3.  In the Join a Workspace dialog box, the available workspaces available on request are listed.
    
4.  Click Request Access corresponding to the workspace you want to join.
