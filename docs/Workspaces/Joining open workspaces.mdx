### Joining open workspaces

1.  On the Home page, from the left navigation panel, click Workspace. The Workspace page, which lists all the workspaces is displayed.
    
2.  From the upper right corner, click the Join button.
    
3.  Open image-20250512-090606.png
    
4.  In the Join a Workspace dialog box, the available workspaces which are open for joining are listed.
    
5.  Open image-20250512-092324.png
    
6.  Click Join corresponding to an available workspace. You can now access the chosen workspace.
