### Downloading data from workbooks

-   Downloading Raw Excel or CSV: Downloads all data exactly as stored, without applying any filters, sorting, column arrangements, or visibility settings from your current view.
    
-   Downloading from a View (Excel or CSV):Downloads data with all current settings applied, including:
    
-   Filters
    
-   Sorting preferences
    
-   Column visibility
    
-   Column order
